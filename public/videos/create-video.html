Creating the Manufacturing Video

To make the hero section video work properly, you need to create a video file named "manufacturing-showcase.mp4" in the public/videos directory.

Option 1: Create from the hero image

If you have FFmpeg installed, you can create a video from the existing hero image with this command:

ffmpeg -loop 1 -i ../images/hero-background.jpg -c:v libx264 -t 10 -pix_fmt yuv420p manufacturing-showcase.mp4

This creates a 10-second loop of the image.

Option 2: Download a free manufacturing video

You can download a free manufacturing video from stock video sites like:

  • Pexels
  • Pixabay
  • Videvo

After downloading, rename it to "manufacturing-showcase.mp4" and place it in the public/videos directory.
